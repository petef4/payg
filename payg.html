PAYG Tariffs

These are the rates for true pay as you go tariffs only. Some other plans are labelled PAYG but require monthly top-ups, etc. Plans which are not open to new customers are also excluded.

Details such as rates for non-geographic numbers (e.g. 0845) are not included here. Discounts are often available when committing to some usage (aka add-ons or bolt-ons). Extra services are available, some of which are free. Follow the links for individual plans to find out more. Some of the better hidden fine print can be found in the plan details links of the operators column.

The five main operators who operate their own networks are O2, Orange, T-Mobile, Vodafone and 3. The first four operate 2G services primarily and are moving towards 3G. 3 operates 3G and uses Orange to provide its 2G coverage. Orange and T-Mobile have announced that they are merging their UK operations as "Everything Everywhere"; users should register to enable roaming. 3, Orange and T-Mobile have a joint venture MBNL to combine their 3G networks.

MVNOs are operators who do not run their own network. The ones who target UK usage are Asda, giffgaff, Family (IKEA), Talkmobile (Carphone Warehouse), Tesco, Utility Warehouse, and Virgin. Those geared towards international plans include GlobalCell, IDT, Lebara, Lycamobile, Nomi, and Vectone. TalkTalk said in July 2010 that they will launch a service in the autumn using Vodafone. That is distinct from CPW's Talkmobile.

The Network column has links to coverage maps. The GSMA also has coverage maps for all the 2G and 3G networks.

The plan which I use is Tesco. giffgaff, Talkmobile, Asda, and Family Mobile are cheaper.

1 3's top ups include some free texts and 150 MB internet allowance, valid for 3 months. Skype is free.

3 Tesco PAYG offer free credit when you top up which doubles or trebles your contribution. The extra lasts for a month.

4 Utility Warehouse ValuePay is subject to a minimum monthly call spend of £1. All calls are subject to a call setup charge of 5p.

5 GlobalCell also offer free credit when you top up which gives an extra 20% to 50%. It is unclear whether that has to be used within a certain time.

6 Lycamobile PLUS also offer free credit when you top up which gives an extra 20% to 50%. It is unclear whether that has to be used within a certain time.

7 Vectone to Vectone calls and text are free for 30 days from top up, to a limit that varies with the amount of the top up.
