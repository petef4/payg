PAYG Tariffs

These are the rates for true pay as you go tariffs only. Some other plans are labelled PAYG but require monthly top-ups, etc. Plans which are not open to new customers are also excluded.

Details such as voice mail and rates for non-geographic numbers (e.g. 0845) are not included here. Discounts are often available when committing to some usage. Extra services are available, some of which are free.

The five main operators who operate their own networks are O2, Orange, T-Mobile and 3. The first four operate 2G services primarily and are moving towards 3G. 3 operates 3G and uses Orange to provide its 2G coverage.

MVNOs are operators who do not run their own network. The ones who target domestic usage are Asda, BT, Family (IKEA), TalkTalk (CPW), Tesco, and Virgin. Those geared towards international plans include GlobalCell, IDT, Lycamobile, Nomi, and Talkmobile (CPW).
