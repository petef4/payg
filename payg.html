PAYG Tariffs

These are the rates for true pay as you go tariffs only. Some other plans are labelled PAYG but require monthly top-ups, etc.

Details such as voice mail and rates for non-geographic numbers (e.g. 0845) are not included here. Discounts are often available when committing to some usage. Extra services are available, some of which are free.

There are other PAYG plans which focus on international calls. They are not listed here.
